DRAGON SUSHI

Ingredients : Dragon sushi is a flavorful and visually stunning sushi roll that typically includes: Protein: Grilled eel (unagi) or shrimp tempura. Fillings: Crab meat, cucumber, and avocado for a creamy and crunchy texture. Toppings: Thinly sliced avocado for a "dragon-scale" look, and unagi sauce for a rich, sweet glaze. Optional Garnishes: Sesame seeds, spicy mayo, or tobiko for added flavor and visual appeal. This sushi roll is a perfect combination of savory, sweet, and umami flavors, making it a favorite for sushi lovers.
